Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

quick and tasty meal that everyone enjoys!


Ingredients

  • Peaniy butter
  • Jelly (many to choose from)
  • Bread of your choice
  • Love

Steps

  1. Get two slices of bread.
  2. apply peanut butter to one slice
  3. apply jelly to other slice
  4. join slice together with peanut butter and jelly facing each other
